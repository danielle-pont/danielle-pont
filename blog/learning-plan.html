End of Foundations Update

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I am really looking forward to a creative job with a diverse set of things to make and create. Hopefully something where I can come up with, or even just help come up with, fresh ideas that both work well and look amazing.


What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

I really enjoy learning new things and I think the Foundations part of this course has satisfied that side of me. While I know Bootcamp is a whole lot more intense and compact, I am excited to learn more. A limitation might be that it sometimes takes me a little bit to pick up a new idea, especially in terms of how things work – but I know my desire to know more will pull me through that.


What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

I’m a bit nervous about working in a group as I really hope I don’t hold anyone back! The cohort all seem really on to things and I would hate to slow the group down in terms of making a great project.


What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I can be really passive about things, especially in a large group. While going with the flow can make things easier, it might be good to get used to bringing forward my own ideas.


What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

I’m hoping for lots of opportunity to ask questions and perhaps even have conversations about how things work.


What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

Using Discord is something I will have to get used to, as I know that will be a great way to put forward any questions I have to either my cohort or the facilitator.
In terms of commitments to managing the workload, I hope to complete everything before the weekend so I have time to go over things at the end of the week. I don’t want to be asking for extensions. I think good planning and sticking to a daily schedule will help here, as well as making sure to have consistent communication with other group members and facilitators.






Beginning of Foundations Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

I hope to work in a role that is always changing so I can come up with completely new ideas on a regular basis. Over the past few months I have been working remotely as a writer for an analytics company and have found that I really love working away from an office – so hopefully I’ll be able to score a job that’s remote.


Describe your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

I am very curious and have always loved coming up with new ideas. I think a limitation that comes along with this, though, is the constant need to move onto the next thing instead of finishing the thing I’m currently doing in a strong way. I’m working on that.


What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

I would love to be able to extend my knowledge base with so many more things that can be done through HTML, CSS, JS and the libraries that come along with them.


A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I want to be able to stick to a schedule and complete everything I need to get done not just on time, but early.
In order to manage a heavier workload, I think scheduling needs to be a priority – as well as sticking to that schedule. However, having good communication is good also in the event that I need to share the load a bit (without just dumping it on someone else, of course!)


How and where you will seek help in a timely way

It will be important for me to first really look for the solution myself while making sure to ask someone if I cannot find the solution in a reasonable amount of time.


What you expect from the facilitation team

I hope to learn as much as I can and to have the opportunity to ask and discuss any questions that come up along the way.


Scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things

I work with a church and so Sundays are normally a very busy day for me. I prefer not to do any other kind of work on this day – unless it’s crucial and important, which I am always happy to do! I’m flexible.
